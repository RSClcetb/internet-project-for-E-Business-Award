Origins

The internet’s origins begin in the 60’s. J.C.R. Licklider first conceptualised a global computer network in 1962.

This idea was shared with his peers at the US department of defense which led to the development of the first wide-area computer network known as ARPANET which was funded by the US Department of defense advanced research projects.

After launching in 1969 the network would grow over the next few years.
